# Housing Filter Research

## Modal Title
"Housing"

## Question
"What type of housing does your target audience have?"

## Filter Structure
Dynamic filter builder (same pattern as Financial/Personal/Family)

## Available Filter Fields

1. **Homeowner Status**
   - Type: Dropdown or boolean
   - Options: (need to determine - likely Own, Rent, Other)

2. **Dwelling Type**
   - Type: Dropdown
   - Options: (need to determine - likely Single Family, Apartment, Condo, Townhouse, etc.)

3. **Year Built**
   - Type: Range (min/max) or dropdown
   - Format: Year

4. **Purchase Price**
   - Type: Range (min/max inputs)
   - Format: Dollar amount

5. **Purchase Year**
   - Type: Range (min/max) or dropdown
   - Format: Year

6. **Estimated Home Value**
   - Type: Range (min/max inputs)
   - Format: Dollar amount

## Action Buttons
- Reset - Clear all housing filters
- Continue - Apply filters and close modal
